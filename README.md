# VI Hub — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 10 July 2026_

VI Hub is an unofficial fan companion app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Rockstar Games or Take-Two Interactive. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

## The short version

VI Hub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to the developer. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Everything the app stores stays on your device.

## Information we collect

**None.** We do not operate a server, and we never receive your data. The app has no login, no user profiles, and no analytics or tracking SDKs.

## Information stored on your device

- **App settings** (theme, feed list, toggles) — saved locally in standard iOS preferences. Never leaves your device.
- **Cached news** — articles fetched from news feeds are cached on your device for offline reading. Cleared when you reset the app or delete it.

## Third-party content the app connects to

To show news and content, the app fetches data directly from third-party sources on your behalf (for example RSS/Atom feeds you configure, and the Rockstar Newswire). When your device requests these, those third parties receive standard network information such as your IP address and device user-agent, under _their own_ privacy policies. We do not control and do not receive that data.

Tapping an article or a community map opens it in an in-app browser; from that point the destination website's own privacy policy applies.

## Notifications

If you enable the launch reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device by iOS. No notification data is sent anywhere.

## Purchases

VI Hub offers an optional one-time "Support the Developer" tip. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details or any personal information. The tip unlocks nothing — it is purely a thank-you and can be given more than once.

## Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
