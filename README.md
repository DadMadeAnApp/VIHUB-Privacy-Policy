<h1>VI Hub — Privacy Policy</h1>
<p class="updated">Last updated: 10 July 2026</p>

<p>VI Hub is an unofficial fan companion app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed
by, or connected to Rockstar Games or Take-Two Interactive. This policy explains
what the app does and does not do with your information.</p>

<h2>The short version</h2>
<p>VI Hub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to the
developer. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.
Everything the app stores stays on your device.</p>

<h2>Information we collect</h2>
<p><strong>None.</strong> We do not operate a server, and we never receive your
data. The app has no login, no user profiles, and no analytics or tracking
SDKs.</p>

<h2>Information stored on your device</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>App settings</strong> (theme, feed list, toggles) — saved locally in
  standard iOS preferences. Never leaves your device.</li>
  <li><strong>Cached news</strong> — articles fetched from news feeds are cached on
  your device for offline reading. Cleared when you reset the app or delete it.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Third-party content the app connects to</h2>
<p>To show news and content, the app fetches data directly from third-party
sources on your behalf (for example RSS/Atom feeds you configure, and the
Rockstar Newswire). When your device requests these, those third parties
receive standard network information such as your IP address and device
user-agent, under <em>their own</em> privacy policies. We do not control and do
not receive that data.</p>
<p>Tapping an article or a community map opens it in an in-app browser; from that
point the destination website's own privacy policy applies.</p>

<h2>Notifications</h2>
<p>If you enable the launch reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device by
iOS. No notification data is sent anywhere.</p>

<h2>Purchases</h2>
<p>VI Hub offers an optional one-time "Support the Developer" tip. Purchases are
processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment
details or any personal information. The tip unlocks nothing — it is purely a
thank-you and can be given more than once.</p>

<h2>Children's privacy</h2>
<p>The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.</p>

<h2>Changes to this policy</h2>
<p>If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a
new "Last updated" date.</p>

<h2>Contact</h2>
<p>Questions about this policy: <a href="mailto:helpdesk@hanel.us">helpdesk@hanel.us</a></p>
